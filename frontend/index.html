Smart Order Router

MiniMatch converts a parent order into deterministic venue-level execution legs using executable depth, effective price, fees, venue health, and available liquidity across Coinbase, Binance, and Kraken.

$ router --capabilities
01

Effective-price ranking

Compares executable prices after venue fees instead of ranking liquidity by displayed price alone.

02

Multi-level depth

Consumes available quantity across multiple price levels until the parent order is filled or liquidity is exhausted.

03

Venue-health filtering

Excludes stale, unsynchronized, or unhealthy venues from new execution plans while preserving their operational telemetry.

04

Deterministic planning

Produces stable, ordered execution legs from the same request, market state, fee model, and health snapshot.

$ router --venue-health-policy
HEALTHY
Venue remains routable

Synchronized books with fresh quotes can contribute liquidity to new route plans.

STALE
Visible but not routable

The venue remains visible in health telemetry, but its quotes are excluded from new routing legs.

INVALID
Update rejected

Sequence gaps, checksum failures, or invalid state prevent the update from reaching the consolidated routing market.

Inputs

  • Consolidated venue books
  • Side and parent quantity
  • Venue fee parameters
  • Price and liquidity levels

Outputs

  • Ordered execution legs
  • Expected average price
  • Estimated fees
  • Filled and remaining quantity

ENGINEERING JOURNAL / 09

Build Log

MiniMatch was developed incrementally: correctness first, then protocols, market connectivity, routing, replay, execution, observability, and measurable performance.

01
foundation

Deterministic matching core

Implemented price-time priority, limit and market orders, IOC behavior, cancellation, partial fills, and execution reports.

02
systems boundary

Binary gateway and event log

Added protocol encoding, TCP transport, replayable events, snapshots, and deterministic recovery tools.

03
market connectivity

Live multi-venue L2

Built adapters for Coinbase, Binance, and Kraken, including snapshots, incremental updates, sequence checks, and checksum handling.

04
execution intelligence

Consolidation and routing

Combined independent venue books and implemented deterministic multi-leg routing with fees and liquidity-aware execution plans.

05
lifecycle

Execution, risk, persistence, and FIX

Added execution algorithms, parent-child order tracking, pre-trade controls, SQLite persistence, FIX sessions, and gateway translation.

06
reproducibility

Binary recording and interactive replay

Captured live sessions and built deterministic replay with play, pause, restart, speed, and seek controls.

07
operations

Health and latency instrumentation

Added venue health, counters, quote age, bounded percentile tracking, route timing, serialization timing, and per-feed apply metrics.

08
deployment

Docker, CI, and observability

Added container builds, automated tests, Prometheus integration, Grafana dashboards, benchmark artifacts, and reproducible startup scripts.
